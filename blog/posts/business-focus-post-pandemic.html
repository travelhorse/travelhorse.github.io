When Should Businesses Focus Post-Pandemic

31 October 2020 / Wednesday / 2:33pm

Introduction

As many cities are experiencing second and third waves of Covid-19 cases, online shopping and online ordering for food continue to be in high demand. Here are a few upcoming online shopping and eating periods to look out for!

Festive Period 1 - Halloween Treats

The time to say “Trick or Treat” ushers in the festive season.

Home-made treats take the front seat this year during Halloween with the surge of online selling. Singapore-based Bakerlette is introducing a Halloween Cheesecake and Brownie Bundle that comes a Halloween plush toy.

The package is delivered complimentary through a dash service (4-hour delivery window) to ensure that products are delivered fresh..

Festive Period 2 - Double 11.11 Sale

Double 11 (11.11. Or 11 Nov), a.k.a Singles’ Day Festival is a global shopping festival that was introduced by Alibaba in 2009.

Since it’s phenomenal boom in China, major e-commerce platforms and retailers have adopted it as one of their strategies to boost sales..

Singles’ Day is a great way to check out creative bundles, offers, packaging and price points to win consumers’ hearts and wallets, in turn increasing consumer lifetime value to brands.

Festive Period 3 - Double 12.12 Sale

If you missed out on Singles’ Day sales, Double 12 may just be your best chance to grab and save for your holiday gift giving spree.

It is also essential to have bespoke dash service (tailored customised luxurious courier service) to ensure that your products will always be delivered at high quality and precision. Instead of having your customers wait at the shop, have your luxurious products delivered to their doorstep.
